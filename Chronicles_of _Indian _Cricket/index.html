◆ A Nation's Journey ◆

The Grand Timeline

🏟️
1932
India's First Test Match

India played their inaugural Test match at Lord's Cricket Ground, London, under the captaincy of C.K. Nayudu — marking the birth of Indian international cricket.

🌟
1952
First Test Series Win

India recorded their first-ever Test series victory against England at home, signalling the rise of a cricketing power that would one day rule the world.

🏆
1983
World Cup Miracle – Kapil's Devils

Under Kapil Dev's inspirational leadership, India shocked the cricketing world by defeating the mighty West Indies in the final at Lord's. A billion hearts erupted.

🏟️
1996
India Co-Hosts World Cup

India, Pakistan and Sri Lanka jointly hosted the ICC Cricket World Cup. The tournament shattered attendance records and cemented cricket's status as a subcontinental religion.

🔵
2007
T20 World Champions

A young side led by MS Dhoni won India's first-ever ICC World Twenty20 title in South Africa, launching the T20 revolution with a nail-biting final against Pakistan.

🏆
2011
World Cup Homecoming

28 years after 1983, MS Dhoni's iconic six at Wankhede sent India into delirium. Sachin Tendulkar — playing his 6th World Cup — finally lifted the trophy.

🌍
2023
50-Over World Cup Finalists

India stormed through the World Cup unbeaten in the league stage before a heartbreaking final loss to Australia in Ahmedabad — but the journey was unforgettable.

🏆
2024
T20 World Champions Again

Rohit Sharma's India clinched the T20 World Cup in the West Indies and USA, defeating South Africa in a thrilling final — ending an 11-year ICC title drought.
